Local Roots

Born in the Heart
of the Garhwal.

"The mountains were never a destination for us; they were our first playground and now, our greatest teacher."

Trek Northern Himalayas didn't start in a boardroom; it started on a ridge at 14,000 feet. Our founders, a trio of local guides from Joshimath, realized that the true Himalayas were being lost to commercial mass-tourism.

We wanted to bring back the "Expedition" feel—where safety is a science, adventure is a lifestyle, and every trekker leaves with a piece of the mountain in their soul.

The Ethos

What We Stand For

Our principles are the anchors for every expedition we launch.

01 / SAFETY

Radical Responsibility

We take full ownership of your experience—from the first message to the final descent. Safety isn't a checklist; it's our core identity.

02 / HERITAGE

Local Inclusion

80% of our revenue goes directly back into the local mountain economy of Chamoli, supporting families who have lived here for centuries.

Pure Adventure

No crowded campsites. No noise. We believe in the raw, unadulterated Himalayan wilderness, preserved for those who truly love the heights.
